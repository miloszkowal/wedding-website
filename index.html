How we met

Before June 17, 2018, we were only connected by a love of classic rock. So when we both ended up at a Roger Daltrey concert where he was performing the entire Tommy soundtrack, it seems a little like fate! We both got our tickets on Groupon, and both almost changed our minds at the last minute. It was instant attraction for both of us and we left that night promising to keep in contact. After several ice cream dates, we were officially dating! The love and support we had for each other kept growing at an alarmingly fast rate, and we knew that we had something special together. We also knew that we wanted to be together for a very long time to come, and that both of us were ready to commit to each other. That’s why on January 1, 2020, with our St. Luke’s family cheering us on, we got engaged! Now the pandemic has made us even more sure of our love for each other. We can’t wait to celebrate this with you all!
